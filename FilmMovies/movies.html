Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Synopsis: Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone tells the story of Harry Potter, an orphan who discovers he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts. He uncovers a plot to steal the Sorcerer's Stone and faces the dark wizard Voldemort.

Director: Chris Columbus

Stars: Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint, Emma Watson

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Synopsis: During his second year at Hogwarts, Harry uncovers the mystery of the legendary Chamber of Secrets after students are mysteriously attacked. With Ron and Hermione, he follows dangerous clues and faces a dark force hidden within the school.

Director: Chris Columbus

Stars: Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint, Emma Watson

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba: Mugen Train

Synopsis: Tanjiro and his friends join the Flame Hashira aboard the Mugen Train to stop a demon behind a string of disappearances. Trapped in enchanted dreams, they must awaken and fight to save the passengers.

Director: Haruo Sotozaki

Stars: Natsuki Hanae, Akari Kito, Hiro Shimono

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Synopsis: In his third year at Hogwarts, Harry learns that the escaped prisoner Sirius Black may be connected to his past. As danger closes in, Harry, Ron, and Hermione uncover hidden truths that change everything they thought they knew.

Director: Alfonso Cuaron

Stars: Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint, Emma Watson

Wall-E

Synopsis: WALL·E is about a small waste-collecting robot left on a deserted Earth who discovers love and a mission to save humanity. WALL·E meets EVE, a sleek probe sent to search for signs of life, and together they embark on a journey across space that inspires humans to return to Earth. The film explores environmental responsibility.

Director: Andrew Stanton

Stars: Ben Burtt, Elissa Knight

Sci-Fi

Interstellar

Synopsis: In a future where Earth is becoming uninhabitable, a former NASA pilot joins a mission through a wormhole to find a new home for humanity, facing impossible choices between saving the world and returning to his family. Interstellar is a science fiction film.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Stars: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain
