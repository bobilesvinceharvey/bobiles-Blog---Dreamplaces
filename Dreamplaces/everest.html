Mount Everest



Why I Want to Visit Mount Everest

Mount Everest represents challenge, discipline, and the desire to push human limits. Even if I do not plan to climb all the way to the summit, visiting the Everest region would already be an unforgettable experience. I want to witness the Himalayas, meet local communities, and see the mountain that has inspired climbers for generations. The scale of Everest reminds people how powerful nature is and how small we are compared to it.

Quick Facts

Mount Everest is the highest mountain above sea level, standing at over 8,800 meters. It is located on the border between Nepal and the Tibet Autonomous Region of China. The mountain is called Sagarmatha in Nepal and Chomolungma in Tibet. Climbers usually approach Everest from either the Nepal side or the Tibet side, and both routes require serious preparation because of extreme weather and high-altitude conditions.
