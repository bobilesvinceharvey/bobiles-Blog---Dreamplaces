Attack on Titan

Synopsis: In a world where humanity lives behind massive walls to survive giant man-eating Titans, Eren Yeager and his friends join the Survey Corps and uncover dark secrets about their world, their enemies, and themselves. By becoming what he hates most, the mysteries slowly unravel.

Stars: Yuki Kaji, Yui Ishikawa, and Marina Inoue

Anime

Your Lie in April

Synopsis: Former piano prodigy Kosei Arima loses his ability to hear his own playing after his mother dies, but his life changes when he meets Kaori Miyazono, a free-spirited violinist who pushes him back into music and helps him face grief, love, and growth.

Director: Kyohei Ishiguro

Stars: Natsuki Hanae, Risa Taneda, Ayane Sakura, Ryota Osaka

Anime

Sword Art Online

Synopsis: Thousands of players are trapped inside a virtual reality MMORPG, and death in the game means death in real life. Kirito fights through dangerous floors and battles to clear the game and return everyone safely to the real world. Sword Art Online is an anime series.

Director: Tomohiko Ito

Stars: Yoshitsugu Matsuoka, Haruka Tomatsu, Kanae Ito

One Piece

Slowly, various people join the crew and its heartwarming and fun adventures in unraveling the mysteries of the world.

Healer

Synopsis: A mysterious night courier known only as “Healer” takes on secretive assignments that require stealth, combat skills, and high-tech hacking. When he’s hired to uncover the truth behind a decades-old incident involving a group of journalists, he crosses paths with a passionate tabloid reporter and a famous star journalist.

Director: Lee Jung-sub & Kim Jin-woo

Stars: Ji Chang-wook, Park Min-young, Yoo Ji-tae

K-Drama

Missing You

Synopsis: Missing You is a romantic melodrama about two childhood sweethearts separated by a traumatic event who meet again as adults, struggling with lingering feelings, and complicated relationships as they search for closure and love. Will they eventually find their way back to each other and find happiness?

Director: Lee Jae-dong & Park Jae-beom

Stars: Park Yoo-chun, Yoon Eun-hye, Yoo Seung-ho

K-Drama

The King: Eternal Monarch

Synopsis: A modern-day Korean emperor crosses into a parallel world and meets a detective. Together, they uncover a conspiracy that links both worlds while trying to close a dangerous gateway between dimensions.They fight to protect their realms while their fates become intertwined.

Director: Baek Sang-hoon, Jung Ji-hyun, Yoo Je-won

K-Drama

Kingdom

Synopsis: Set in Korea's Joseon era, a crown prince uncovers a terrifying plague that turns people into flesh-hungry monsters. As political betrayal spreads across the palace, he must fight to save his people and uncover the truth behind the outbreak. The struggle for survival becomes a desperate race to protect the nation from total collapse.

Director: Kim Seong-hun

Stars: Ju Ji-hoon, Bae Doona, Ryu Seung-ryong
